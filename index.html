Important: real diagonal gradients, wavy, bounce, shake, spin and scale need rendertype_text.vsh. Your current rendertype_text.fsh can only recolor pixels; it cannot move glyph vertices.

For TheSalt-style diagonal text, the VSH colors every corner of the glyph differently and the GPU interpolates the color across the letter.
